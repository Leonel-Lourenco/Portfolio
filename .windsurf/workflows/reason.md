## Objective
Analyze the problem deeply and identify the most logical solution.

## Requirements
- Examine the problem from multiple angles and perspectives
- Identify root causes, not just symptoms
- Evaluate potential solutions for feasibility, scalability, and maintainability
- Consider edge cases and potential side effects
- Propose the most logical, elegant solution with clear implementation steps

## Deliverable
Provide a clear analysis of:
1. Problem statement and root cause
2. Why this is the most logical solution
3. Step-by-step implementation approach
